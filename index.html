Reducing the inter scanner variability in Activation

To reduce the inter-scanner differences in multi-center fMRI data by using LSTM. In this study the data corresponding to subject 106 where each subject visited the scanners at least twice, and 4 runs per visit. The sensors -motor task contained 85 time points. The scaled time series data of the 1.5T and 4T is considered for training and testing. The model is trained to predict the 4T time series data from the 1.5T time series. LSTM is used to predict the 4T scans from the 1.5T scans, thus reducing the inter-scanner variability in multi-site fMRI data

Global Wheat Detection

I have useda large, diverse and well-labelled dataset of wheat images, called the Global Wheat Head Detection(GWHD) dataset.
